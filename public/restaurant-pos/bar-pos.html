More on Bar POS

Perhaps the best feature of your Bar POS is that it is Cloud-based. The advantage of using a cloud POS over an on-premise POS is that the software keeps getting auto-updated. You do not have to replace your system as the Bar POS will constantly be upgraded in line with the latest technology. That way you do not have to incur heavy fixed costs repeatedly.

Managing a bar is not easy. Your bartenders are always on their toes, serving clients, taking and managing orders. With a bar cloud POS system, you can significantly improve your bar's efficiency. Multiple tabs can now be easily managed or transferred at the same time.
Split checks are now possible at the table itself. Orders can be quickly and effectively managed from a single central system and menu changes can be made on the go. Moreover, this does not require you to be physically present in the bar.
